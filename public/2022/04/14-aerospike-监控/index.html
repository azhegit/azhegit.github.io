14-aerospike 监控

2022 年 四月 18 号 发布在 技术, 数据库

prometheus跟grafana已经安装好，此篇忽略，重点讲Aerospike监控安装及配置。

  1. 下载aerospike-prometheus-exporter https://download.aerospike.com/artifacts/aerospike-prometheus-exporter/1.5.0/aerospike-prometheus-exporter-1.5.0-x86_64.rpm.tar.gz
  2. 安装 rpm -ivh aerospike-prometheus-exporter-1.5.0-x86_64.rpm
  3. 启动aerospike-prometheus-exporter systemctl start aerospike-prometheus-exporter.service
  4. 验证是否启动端口 telnet localhost 9145
  5. 配置Prometheus
scrape_configs:
  - job_name: 'aerospike'
    static_configs:
      - targets: ['aerospike-1:9145']
      - targets: ['aerospike-2:9145']
      - targets: ['aerospike-3:9145']
      - targets: ['aerospike-4:9145']
